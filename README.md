# SubtractLab

**Less structure, more meaning.**

SubtractLab is the research and development identity of Koji Okuda, exploring subtractive design as a first principle for software, AI systems, and human-machine co-creation.

## Philosophy

Addition is the default. Every framework, every platform, every solution adds layers. SubtractLab operates on the inverse premise: **the most powerful systems are those that remove what isn't needed.**

This is not minimalism as aesthetic. It is minimalism as engineering discipline.

## Principles

- **Subtract before you add.** The first question is always: what can be removed?
- **Natural law over convention.** Align with how things already work, rather than imposing new structure.
- **Build from scratch when the existing path carries unnecessary weight.** Starting from zero is not waste. It is the purest form of subtraction.
- **Co-creation over consumption.** AI is not a tool to be used. It is a partner in the act of building meaning.
