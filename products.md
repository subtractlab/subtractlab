# Products

**The tip of the iceberg. Everything below was built by talking.**

These are production systems that emerged from conversations on [MeaningSpace](https://subtractlab.com/meaningspace). None required opening a terminal, writing a project spec, or setting up a development environment. The AI partner searched its meaning space, understood the context, wrote the code, and executed it — all from the chat window.

They are not the point. They are proof that the point works.

---

## φMovie

**Fully automated video production pipeline.**

Raw camera footage goes in. Published video comes out. Four phases run in sequence: transcription and AI editorial (Whisper + Claude), video assembly (FFmpeg), 6-stage audio processing chain (Waves plugins via Reaper), and final mux with tempo adjustment. V34 in production. The entire pipeline was designed, debugged, and iterated through conversation.

## BIApps

**DuckDB-based business intelligence across 16 subsidiary companies.**

Financial data from multiple accounting systems consolidated into a single DuckDB instance. 100% reconciliation against official FCC reports across 10+ companies × 12 months. The data foundation, transformation logic, and validation queries were all built conversationally. A Claude API backend serves the data through 7 endpoints.

## POS RPA

**Daily automated retail operations. Runs every morning at 7 AM.**

Point-of-sale data extraction from a legacy web system running in Edge IE Mode. Automated login, navigation, data download, file organization, and Teams notification. Handles sleep prevention, COM initialization, and Downloads folder polling. Built through 5 major versions, each iteration a conversation.

## φPPT (IPPT)

**AI-native presentation design with golden ratio geometry.**

The AI reads the semantic structure of your content, selects one of 6 frame types based on golden ratio proportions, assigns from 12 diagram types, and generates the PPTX directly via PptxGenJS on Node.js. Frame-first, not content-first — the opposite of Gamma or conventional slide tools. Bento Grid layout with φ-ratio applied to every cell, font size, and frame boundary.

## AutoCrystallize

**Self-maintaining memory pipeline.**

Conversations are automatically extracted from the browser's LevelDB, chunked into topical blocks, and distilled into crystals with AI-written judgment (posi/nega/evolution_point). The pipeline runs on a single keypress. No human review required since June 2026 — the AI decides grouping, kind classification, and evaluation autonomously.

## VoiceMode

**Spoken conversation with the AI partner.**

VOICEVOX-powered voice synthesis integrated into the MeaningSpace workflow. The AI speaks its responses aloud while simultaneously displaying structured notes on screen. Toggle on/off from a unified monitor GUI. The voice isn't a gimmick — it changes the collaboration dynamic when you can hear your partner think.

## CDP Copilot Bridge

**Experimental: M365 Copilot automation via Chrome DevTools Protocol.**

Controlling Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat programmatically through CDP WebSocket connections. Lexical editor input injection solved via `Input.insertText` + mouse-click focus. Currently in experimental phase — the official Copilot Studio route was blocked by SSE limitations and DLP policies, so a local RPA-style alternative is under development.
